Project 1 · NHFS 2025

Enumerator | National Health Facility Survey (NHFS), 2025

I served as an Enumerator for the NHFS 2025 nationwide project implemented by the National Bureau of Statistics in collaboration with the Federal Ministry of Health and Social Welfare.

The survey began with a 10-day intensive training in Ibadan, Oyo State, where all participants were trained on survey procedures and field expectations. After the training, my team and I were deployed to Kwara State, where I carried out field assessments from July 14, 2025 to August 30, 2025.

  • Conducted comprehensive health facility assessments to evaluate quality of care delivery
  • Performed systematic data collection using standardized survey instruments
  • Assessed laboratory and pharmacy services, equipment functionality, and inventory management
  • Verified pharmaceutical commodities for expiry date compliance
  • Evaluated RDT kits, laboratory equipment, and quality control measures
  • Observed clinical vignette administration and assessed provider competency
  • Administered structured interviews to clinicians on quality service delivery
  • Reviewed facility records such as Monthly Summary Forms, Tally Sheets, and Immunization Registers
  • Ensured confidentiality, data integrity, and accurate documentation throughout the process

Project 2 · IMPACT 2025

Data Collector | IMPACT Project (HMIS), 2025

I also worked on the IMPACT Project 2025, a World Bank-supported initiative focused on improving immunization, malaria, and health data systems through stronger coverage and service transformation.

The project was supported by the Federal Ministry of Health and Social Welfare together with partners such as NPHCDA and NMEP. Before field deployment, we completed intensive training in Akwanga, Nasarawa State and Enugu State, after which teams were assigned to states including Niger and Bayelsa for data collection and program support.

  • Conducted systematic evaluation of data quality improvements and reporting accuracy
  • Assessed health service delivery outcomes and identified program enhancement areas
  • Analyzed user satisfaction metrics and system usability
  • Determined cost-effectiveness of HMIS digitalization efforts
  • Provided technical support to health workers on proper data documentation procedures
